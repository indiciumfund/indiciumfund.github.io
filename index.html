Developing index solutions for a volatile crypto market

Indicium is a blockchain project designed with the volatility of the cryptocurrency marketplace in mind. Formed by a decentralized community of like-minded individuals, Indicium uses the PeerAssets protocol hosted on the Peercoin blockchain to organize and facilitate communication. Peerasset tokens spawned in association with Indicium are for organization purposes and have no expectation of value and should not be sold or resold on cryptocurrency exchanges or otherwise. The goals of the project are three-fold:

  1. Expanding on a common interest in indexing cryptocurrencies.

  2. Organizing using the Peercoin blockchain in ways that may be ported to any decentralized effort.

  3. Becoming a part of the global social and legal structure of the cryptocurrency community.
